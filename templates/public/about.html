The Problem

Democracy Doesn't Fail at Voting. It Fails After.

Elections happen once every few years. Governance happens every day. Yet citizens have almost no visibility into decisions, promises, or performance once voting ends.

No Vote Verification
Citizens can't independently confirm their ballot was recorded and counted correctly
No Post-Election Accountability
Once elected, representatives face no structured mechanism to track or measure their performance until the next election
Unresolved Civic Issues
Citizens have no formal channel to raise local problems, track resolution, or hold anyone accountable for outcomes
Our Solution

Blockchain Proof Meets Continuous Governance

Technology Stack

Three Pillars of Trust

Blockchain immutability, Merkle-proof cryptography, and AI-driven intelligence — working together to make democratic governance transparent and continuously accountable

Ethereum Blockchain

Votes are recorded as immutable smart contract events on the Ethereum Sepolia testnet. No central authority can alter, delete, or tamper with any record — the ledger is public and permanent.

Merkle Proof Receipts

Each vote generates an anonymous receipt hash. After election close, a Merkle root is published on-chain — allowing any voter to cryptographically prove their vote was included, without revealing how they voted.

AI Governance Engine

An ML model generates real-time constituency briefs every hour. A multi-dimensional scoring system evaluates representatives daily — and policy posts are AI-assessed for factual integrity and balanced discourse.

How It Works

From Enrollment to Accountability

1
Step 01

ERO Enrollment

The Electoral Registration Officer verifies your identity and adds you to the official constituency electoral roll. Draft and final rolls are published before election day — no self-registration exists, ensuring only verified citizens participate.

2
Step 02

Booth Voting

On election day, a Presiding Officer authorizes you at a secure, single-device booth terminal. Your encrypted ballot is cast and recorded directly as a smart contract event on the Ethereum blockchain — anonymous and tamper-proof.

3
Step 03

Merkle Proof Verification

After the election closes, a Merkle tree of all receipt hashes is built and its root published on-chain. Use your personal receipt hash and Merkle proof to independently verify your vote was counted — no trust in any authority required.

4
Step 04

Civic Governance

Between elections, raise local constituency issues with photo evidence, follow acceptance and resolution timelines, comment on representative policy posts, and confirm when problems are actually fixed — your feedback feeds directly into your rep's performance score.

5
Step 05

Continuous Accountability

AI scores every representative daily across four dimensions — accountability, engagement, integrity, and impact. Scores are historically tracked over 90-day windows, and a representative whose performance falls below threshold can have their term terminated before the next election.
